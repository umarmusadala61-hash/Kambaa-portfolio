Kambaa Photos is the studio of Alameen Kabir Usman, a photographer based in Dutse, Jigawa State, working across portraits, ceremonies and everyday moments worth remembering.

The work leans toward warmth over polish — real expressions, natural light where possible, and a patient approach to getting the frame right rather than fixing it later. Every shoot starts with a conversation, not a checklist.

Clients range from individuals wanting a proper portrait to families and couples marking a moment in their lives. Each session is treated the same way: with care, attention, and a commitment to images that still mean something years later.
